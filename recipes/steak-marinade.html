Recipe for steak marinade

Description

This marinade combines Balsamic vinegar, Wortcestershire sauce, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, and garlic.

This recipe can be used with rib steaks, but it can work with any cut of beef. This marinade can take at least two hours.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup Balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ cup Wortcestershire sauce
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Wortcestershire sauce, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, and garlic in a small bowl.
  2. Marinate for at least 2 hours
  3. Cook in oven and enjoy!
